About the Restaurant

Founded in 1998 in the heart of Florence, La Bella Tavola brings the warmth and flavor of traditional Italian dining to your table. Located at 45 Via Roma, Florence, Italy, our restaurant specializes in authentic Italian cuisine made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients. At La Bella Tavola, our passion is sharing the true taste of Italy with every guest. We are dedicated to crafting meals that celebrate family, flavor, and freshness. Our goal is simple: to make every meal unforgettable, from the first bite to the last sip of espresso.
